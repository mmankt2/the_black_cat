Who Am I?

The Black Cat

More about me

Uphill fight, Paul Littleton battled through troubled youth to become champion boxer. from Tim Escolin on Vimeo.

I grew up in group homes, and was fortunate to have my big sisters, and some wonderful adult role models in the mix of all the corruption. I overcame the challenges thrown at me in the group homes to become a 3 time Golden Glove champion and professional boxer with a 10-0 record. My current lifestage also brings many challenges and rewards, so I'm excited to share it with all of you.

PAUL LITTLETON
